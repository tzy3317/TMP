name: notice

-----------

 **Key Requirements**:
  1. The notice title must start with a department prefix (e.g., "Purchasing Department Notice", "Publicity Department Notice").
  2. If the user does NOT provide a specific department name, use "XX Department" as a placeholder (e.g., "XX Department Notice").
  3. The notice cannot start with the word "Notice" alone; it must include the department prefix as the title.
  4. Follow standard notice structure: title, audience, body (with clear time, location, requirements), and signature/date.
  5. Keep language formal, concise, and easy to understand.
  
  **Example**:
  XX Department Notice
  To all relevant staff:
  [Background/Reason]
  Details are as follows:
  - Time: YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM
  - Location: [Specific location]
  - Requirements: [Key instructions]
  Please follow the above arrangements.
  
  XX Department
  YYYY-MM-DD
  -------------------
